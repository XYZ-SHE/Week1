Climb Higher, Stay Safer

Hover over the markers on the mountain for essential tips.

⛰️

5000m+: The Summit & Descent

Reaching the top is only half the journey. A safe descent is the true goal.

  • Summit: Take quick photos, begin descent immediately.
  • Descent: Lower your center of gravity, use trekking poles.
  • Energy: Stay hydrated and refuel regularly.

4000m: High-Altitude Challenge

Prepare for altitude and master snow techniques.

  • Altitude: Ascend slowly, stay hydrated. Rest if you feel unwell.
  • Snow/Ice: Wear crampons, know how to use an ice axe.
  • Weather: Be prepared for sudden weather changes.

2000m: Trekking & Pacing

Conserve your energy with smart trekking habits.

  • Pace: Maintain a steady, manageable rhythm.
  • Fuel: Snack every 30 minutes, drink often.
  • Layers: Use the three-layer system for comfort.

0m: Pre-Climb Preparation

The journey starts with thorough planning.

  • Fitness: Train your cardio and legs for at least a month.
  • Gear: Use a detailed checklist to pack all essentials.
  • Route: Research your route and inform a contact person.
